Provider Store x Artificer

A special collaboration between Provider Store, Artificer Coffee and Ceramic Studio En.

With the cup made by Ceramic Studio En and the pure soy wax poured by Provider Store, once it burns out the vessel is designed to be used as an Artificer Coffee cup or brought back in store for a refill.

The packaging exhibits delicate labels on the front and back that are subtle yet refined. The hand torn labels printed on rice paper create a personalised feel.
