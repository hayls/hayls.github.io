Australian Centre for the Moving Image Flyer

‘Celebrating Japan’s Finest Animations of the 21st Century’ is a make-believe event created in response to a brief for a chosen not-for-profit organisation’s event flyer. The design is an homage to traditional Japanese design principles—symbolic, simple yet beautiful, thoughtful and multipurpose. This foldable flyer doubles as an event poster on the back when opened up and the inside reveals the event details, encouraging interactivity through its structural composition and layout.

Risograph printed using soft, off-white recycled paper.
