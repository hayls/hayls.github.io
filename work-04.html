Provider x Goodhood

A limited edition ceramic candle collaboration between Provider Store and Goodhood.

Goodhood London is a minimalist retail store for designer male and female streetwear and homeware.

The belly band packaging label thoughtfully marries Provider Store’s simple and refined aesthetic with Goodhood London’s sophisticated streetwear aesthetic.

Printed on thick, smooth black paper using white ink.
